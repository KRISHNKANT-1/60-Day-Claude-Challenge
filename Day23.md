🚀 New Skill: Customer & MVP Blueprint
Turn a raw startup idea into a full customer-research + MVP scoping report — built as a Claude Skill.
What it does
Acts as a Startup Product Manager + Customer Research Expert. Give it a startup idea (or an existing validation report) and it generates:

Ideal Customer Profile & Buyer Persona
Top 10 Customer Pain Points
Customer Journey (Awareness → Consideration → Purchase → Retention)
Key Objections & Buying Triggers
MVP Recommendation — what to build first, what not to build, success metrics
MoSCoW Prioritization
Pricing Hypothesis
Top 5 Risks
30-Day MVP Plan
Founder Action Sheet (top 10 next steps)
Scores (0–100): Customer Clarity, Problem Severity, PMF Potential, MVP Readiness
Final Verdict: 🟢🟡🟠🔴 demand signal rating
