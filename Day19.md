A multi-stage Football Intelligence experience powered by Claude AI.
Fed it a workbook, asked it to predict the World Cup, quiz my football knowledge, and match my personality to either Messi or Ronaldo. It did all three — in one session.

## My results
- ⚽ World Cup 2026 pick: **Argentina** (78% confidence)
- 📊 Football Awareness Score: **40/100** — Casual Viewer
- ⚡ Personality Archetype: **Relentless Competitor**
- 🐐 Legend Match: **Ronaldo 55%** · Messi 45%

## What surprised me
I scored 100% on Rules and Tournaments but 0% on Tactics, Formations, and Analytics.
Apparently I know football exists but have no idea how it actually works tactically.
High ambition (9/10), low xG awareness (0/10). Classic Relentless Competitor problem.

## Prompt structure
- Stage 0 → Knowledge level check
- Stage 1 → WC 2026 prediction report from workbook data
- Stage 2 → 5-question Football IQ Quiz
- Stage 3 → 12-question Messi vs Ronaldo personality quiz
- Final → Unified Football Intelligence Profile

## Tech
Built entirely in Claude.ai — no code, no API, just a well-structured prompt and a fake workbook PDF.
All visualisations rendered as interactive HTML widgets inside the chat.
