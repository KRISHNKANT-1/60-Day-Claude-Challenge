 Digital Footprint & Privacy Dashboard

An interactive, single-file HTML dashboard that analyzes a user's app list and 
generates an estimated digital footprint score, privacy score, exposure heatmap, 
company exposure ranking, data collection matrix, risk radar, and a privacy 
improvement plan.

Built as part of a 60-day Claude challenge — every insight in the dashboard is 
clearly labeled as either a **Fact** (the apps you actually listed) or an 
**Estimate** (AI-inferred behavior, risk, and scoring). No private databases, 
no real account access — just transparent, rule-based inference.
